Genreal English courses

Our General English courses are our most popular option and will help you improve in all areas of the English Language: grammar, vocabulary, speaking, listening, pronunciation, reading and writing. All our courses also integrate 21st Century Skills into their curriculum. The minimum age for individuals is 16, but we occasionallyaccept 15-year-olds in General English international classes if they are part of a group with a group leader. We can accept 14-year-olds only in closed groups with a group leader.

IELTS Preparation

IELTS or International English Language Testing System assesses the English language proficiency of people who want to study or work where English is used as the language of communication. Our IELTS with pre-sessional English courses not only prepare students for the IELTS exam, but also give guidance on academic study techniques for use at an English-speaking university. Please note that the 4-week IELTS Express course does not include a pre-sessional English language component while the 6 and 10-week courses do. We also have a range of online IELTS courses, both full-time and part-time. The fee for the IELTS Academic Module is £180.

Social Life

Social Programme

London is one of the most exciting cities in the world and you will never be bored if you come to us. We want you to have fun, make friends, speak English and enjoy all that England has to offer, so we organise an exciting and inclusive social programme. A large variety of sports and activities which take place in school are completely free. For some excursions and activities, you will only need to pay the cost of your travel and for others, there may be a charge. For our WSE Live! online courses, we have a range of daily social programme activities which allow our students to socialise together and practise their English from the comfort of their own home or office.
